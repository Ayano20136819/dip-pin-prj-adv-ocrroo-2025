## Persona 1

```markdown
# Persona: [Oliver]

## Background
Give the person's background - make sure we can understand their level of skills, knowledge, and experience.

He has been blind since birth. He has been studying programming course.

## Goals
Why does this person use the application? What are they trying to achieve?

He wants to work from home, and likes problem-solving, which are his motivations for studying programming.
He wants to try sample code from tutorial video.

## Needs
What does this person need from the application? What are their pain points?

As Oliver,
I want to save the codes to files from the video
So that I can try the sample code in the file and it helps me to understand easily.

He cannot know when he should get a frame from video to save all the code.
He needs to adjust time, and start and pause a video. 
```
